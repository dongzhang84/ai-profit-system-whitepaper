# Cover Image Prompt

## Visual concept

A minimalist typography cover where the Chinese title itself IS the visual. The cover is an article header for a Chinese long-form piece recommending 3 Claude Code skills to every reader. The mood is quiet, confident, editorial — no gimmicks, no illustrations, no decorative icons beyond the absolute minimum.

## Canvas

- 16:9 aspect ratio
- Background: pure off-white (#FAFAFA or similar, not harsh pure white)
- 60%+ whitespace / breathing room
- Monochrome palette only: off-white background + near-black text (#1A1A1A) + one subtle light gray accent (#D0D0D0) if needed for a divider or small mark

## Typography (the hero element)

- Primary Chinese title exactly as given, rendered in a clean, modern sans-serif Chinese typeface (think Source Han Sans / Noto Sans CJK SC, heavy or extra-bold weight)
- Title text: **每个人都应该使用的三个最有用的 Claude Skill**
- The numeral "三" may optionally be visually emphasized (larger weight, or a different hairline treatment) to draw the eye to the "3" in the title — but keep it subtle
- "Claude Skill" set in a matching clean sans-serif Latin face (e.g., Inter, Söhne)
- Title left-aligned in the left-center of the canvas, NOT centered
- Generous line spacing; wrap the title across 2 lines at a natural break (suggested break: "每个人都应该使用的" / "三个最有用的 Claude Skill")

## Minimal accent

One single minor mark to anchor the composition, no more. Choose ONE:

- A small forward slash "/" in light gray near the title, hinting at the `/skill-name` syntax of Claude Code skills
- OR a thin horizontal rule (1px light gray) under the title, spanning only part of the width
- OR a small numeral "3" set in large outline type in the upper right corner, very faint (10-15% opacity)

Do NOT add: icons, illustrations, figures, decorative shapes, gradients, shadows, or any 3D effects.

## Composition rules

- Visual anchor: the title block itself, left-center
- Whitespace: at least 60% of the canvas should be empty off-white
- No borders, no frames
- No watermark, no author name, no date
- Flat vector aesthetic: sharp edges, no blur, no texture

## What to avoid

- No realistic humans, no cartoon characters
- No robots, no AI imagery, no neural nets, no chatbot icons
- No code windows, no terminals, no brackets as decoration
- No gradient backgrounds, no noise, no film grain
- No emoji

## Output

- `cover.png`, 16:9, minimum 1600×900 (preferred 2400×1350 for retina)

## Reference aesthetic

Editorial book cover minimalism — think the cover of a Penguin Classics reissue, or the typographic covers of *Wired* essays, or a Muji product catalog: all white space, precise type, no decoration.
